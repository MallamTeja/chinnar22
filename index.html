About Us

Established in 1984, Chinar Scientific Industries is a government-approved, ISO 9001:2015-certified organization. We specialize in setting up STEM labs, ICT solutions, training, and turnkey educational project execution across India.

Our Core Business

We work as resellers, implementing agencies, and system integrators for government IT projects.

We serve departments under the Ministry of Education, Ministry of Science & Technology, Ministry of Health, and Ministry of Women & Child Development — delivering end-to-end ICT lab infrastructure, devices, and training.
